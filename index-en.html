About us

Tradition, taste and nature in the heart of Kaylaka.

Nestled in the beautiful nature of the protected area of Kaylaka, Kosharite Restaurant offers a peaceful atmosphere and food for true connoisseurs. We believe that great food brings people together, which is why we prepare every dish with attention to detail and a love for Bulgarian culinary traditions. Our menu includes charcoal grill specialties, fresh salads and homemade dishes that turn every visit into a memorable experience. Whether you are joining us for a relaxed dinner, a festive occasion or simply a refreshing beer in nature, you will find a warm welcome, a cozy atmosphere and flavors worth remembering.
