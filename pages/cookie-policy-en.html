Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2025

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember your actions and preferences for a certain period of time.

Cookies cannot run programs or transmit viruses. They are completely passive and serve only to transfer information.

How do we use cookies?

The Restaurant Kosharite website uses a minimal number of cookies, divided into two categories:

Type Provider Purpose Duration
Required kosharite.bg Stores your cookie preferences (cc_cookie) 12 months
Analytics Google Analytics Anonymous statistics — number of visitors, pages viewed, session duration. Data is not linked to personal information. Up to 2 years

Important: Analytics cookies are activated only after your explicit consent. If you decline, no analytics cookies will be stored on your device.

Third-party cookies

With your consent, Google Analytics (owned by Google LLC) places cookies for statistical purposes. This data is processed according to the Google Privacy Policy.

Restaurant Kosharite does not sell or share cookie data with advertising networks or third parties for marketing purposes.

Managing cookies

You can change your preferences at any time using the button below or the cookie icon in the bottom-left corner of the website.

You can also manage or delete cookies directly from your browser settings:

Chrome · Firefox · Safari · Edge

Please note that blocking all cookies may cause some website features to not function properly.
